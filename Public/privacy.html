Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Welcome to RewardHub ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

📋 1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information needed to provide our service:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google.
  • Points & activity: Your earned points, claimed rewards, and ad-watching history are stored so your progress is saved across devices.
  • Device identifiers: Our ad partner (Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID) for ad personalisation and analytics, as described in their own privacy policy.

🔧 2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account and sync your points balance.
  • To display personalised rewards and offers.
  • To show advertisements through Google AdMob and award you points for watching them.
  • To send you email notifications about important app updates (optional).

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for any purpose beyond operating the RewardHub service.

🔐 3. Google Sign-In

RewardHub uses Google Sign-In powered by Firebase Authentication. When you sign in with your Google account:

  • Google shares your email address and name with us.
  • We store this information in Firebase (Google Cloud) to create your account. Firebase's privacy policy applies: firebase.google.com/support/privacy .
  • We do not access your Google contacts, drive, calendar, or any other Google service.

📢 4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

RewardHub displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies, device identifiers, and other technologies to serve relevant ads.

  • AdMob's use of data is governed by Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy .
  • You can opt out of personalised ads on Android by going to Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID.
  • Watching a rewarded ad earns you points. No personal data is shared with advertisers beyond what AdMob collects as part of standard ad delivery.

🔒 5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore (cloud database). Firebase uses industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.

  • Access to your data is restricted to your account only.
  • We use Firebase Security Rules to prevent unauthorised access.
  • We regularly review our security practices.

🗑️ 6. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete your account: Go to the RewardHub app → Profile → Delete Account. This permanently deletes your email, points balance, and all activity history from our systems.
  • Access your data: Contact us at the email below and we will provide a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correct your data: You can update your email by re-signing in with a different Google account.

We will process all deletion requests within 30 days.

👶 7. Children's Privacy

RewardHub is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

📝 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.

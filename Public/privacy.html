Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Welcome to RewardHub ("we", "our", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

By using RewardHub, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the app and delete your account.

📋 1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data needed to operate the service:

  • Account info: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address.
  • App activity: Points balance, rewards claimed, and ad-watching history — so your progress is saved across devices.
  • Device identifiers: Google AdMob (our ad partner) may collect your Android Advertising ID for ad personalisation, as described in Google's own privacy policy.

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, or any other data from your device.

🔧 2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account and sync your points balance.
  • To display personalised rewards and offers.
  • To serve advertisements via Google AdMob and credit you with points for completing rewarded ads.
  • To improve the app (anonymised aggregate analytics only).

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers beyond what AdMob requires for standard ad delivery.

🔐 3. Google Sign-In

RewardHub uses Google Sign-In powered by Firebase Authentication.

  • Google shares your email and display name with us when you sign in.
  • We store this in Firebase (Google Cloud). Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy.
  • We request only the profile and email scopes. We do not access your Gmail, Drive, Calendar, or any other Google service.

📢 4. Advertising — Google AdMob

RewardHub earns revenue by showing advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies and device identifiers to serve relevant ads.

  • AdMob's data practices are governed by Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.
  • To opt out of personalised ads on Android, go to Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID.
  • Watching a rewarded ad earns you points. No additional personal data is shared with individual advertisers.

Our AdMob Publisher ID: pub-1560161047680443

🔒 5. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database protected by:

  • TLS encryption in transit.
  • Encryption at rest.
  • Firebase Security Rules that restrict each user to their own data only.

No employee or third party can read your individual account data without your consent, except as required by law.

🗑️ 6. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have full control:

  • Delete your account: Open the RewardHub app → Profile → Delete Account. This permanently and immediately removes your email, points, and all history from our systems.
  • Request your data: Email us and we will send you a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Correct your data: Re-sign in with a different Google account to update your email.

All requests are processed within 30 days.

👶 7. Children's Privacy

RewardHub is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

🌍 8. International Users

RewardHub is operated from Jordan. By using the app you consent to your data being processed in accordance with this policy. We comply with applicable data protection laws including GDPR where relevant.

📝 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. The "Last updated" date at the top always shows the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
